Yoga

Yoga is a holistic system for attaining spiritual realization. The word "Yoga" means union — the union of the individual self with the universal self.

What is Yoga?

According to Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, yoga is "chitta vritti nirodha" — the cessation of the fluctuations of the mind. Yoga is not merely physical exercise but a complete path to self-realization.

Paths of Yoga

Raja Yoga — The royal path of meditation and ethical discipline
Karma Yoga — The path of selfless action
Bhakti Yoga — The path of devotion
Jnana Yoga — The path of knowledge
