  <h1>Vedanta</h1>

  <p>Vedanta is one of the six systems of Indian philosophy. The word Vedanta literally means "the end of the Vedas" and refers to the philosophical teachings found in the Upanishads.</p>

  <h2>What is Vedanta?</h2>
  <p>Vedanta teaches the fundamental truth that:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>The individual self (Atman) is identical with the ultimate reality (Brahman)</li>
    <li>What we perceive as the world is ultimately an illusion (Maya)</li>
    <li>Liberation (Moksha) comes from realizing this truth</li>
  </ul>
