  <h1>Yoga</h1>

  <p>Yoga is a holistic system for attaining spiritual realization. The word "Yoga" means union — the union of the individual self with the universal self.</p>

  <h2>What is Yoga?</h2>
  <p>According to Patanjali's Yoga Sutras, yoga is "chitta vritti nirodha" — the cessation of the fluctuations of the mind. Yoga is not merely physical exercise but a complete path to self-realization.</p>

  <h2>Paths of Yoga</h2>
  <div class="credentials">
    <div class="credential-item"><strong>Raja Yoga</strong> — The royal path of meditation and ethical discipline</div>
    <div class="credential-item"><strong>Karma Yoga</strong> — The path of selfless action</div>
    <div class="credential-item"><strong>Bhakti Yoga</strong> — The path of devotion</div>
    <div class="credential-item"><strong>Jnana Yoga</strong> — The path of knowledge</div>
  </div>
